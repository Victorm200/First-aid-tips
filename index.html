⛑️ Quick First Aid Tips

🫀 CPR

1. Check responsiveness.
2. Call for help.
3. Start chest compressions – 100–120 per minute.
4. Give rescue breaths if trained.

🔥 Burns

1. Cool the burn with clean running water for 10–20 minutes.
2. Remove tight items (rings, watches) from the area.
3. Cover with a clean, non-stick cloth or plastic wrap.
4. Never apply oil, butter, or ice.
5. Seek help for large or severe burns.

🐍 Snake Bite

1. Keep the person calm and still to slow the spread of venom.
2. Do NOT suck the wound or cut it.
3. Keep the bite area below heart level.
4. Remove tight items like jewelry near the bite.
5. Take the person to a hospital immediately.

👃 Nosebleeds

1. Sit upright and lean slightly forward.
2. Pinch the nose just below the bridge for 10 minutes.
3. Breathe through the mouth.
4. Avoid lying down or tilting head back.
5. Seek help if bleeding lasts more than 20 minutes.

💔 Heart Attack

1. Call emergency services immediately.
2. Help the person sit and stay calm.
3. Loosen tight clothing.
4. If trained, give CPR if the person becomes unresponsive.
5. Don’t let them eat or drink anything.

⚡ Electric Shock

1. Do NOT touch the person until the power source is off.
2. Call emergency services immediately.
3. Check for breathing and pulse.
4. Start CPR if needed and trained.
5. Treat visible burns carefully.

🤢 Poisoning

1. Do NOT make the person vomit unless told by medical staff.
2. Check the label of the substance if available.
3. Move them to fresh air if inhaled poison.
4. Call emergency services or poison control.
5. Keep the person calm and still.

🩸 Bleeding

1. Apply pressure with cloth.
2. Keep injured part raised.
3. Don’t remove soaked cloth – add more layers.
4. Call emergency services.

😵 Fainting

1. Help the person lie down and raise their legs.
2. Loosen tight clothing.
3. Make sure they get fresh air.
4. If unconscious for more than 1 minute, call help.

😮‍💨 Choking

1. Ask if they can speak.
2. Give 5 back blows between shoulder blades.
3. Follow with 5 abdominal thrusts.
4. Repeat if needed.
